# Retail Sales SQL Analytics System (Olist Brazil)

## 💡 Executive Business Insights

### 1. High-Value Product Dependency Risk
* **Insight:** The Technology and Watch categories generate the highest margin contribution despite lower relative order volume. 
* **Implication:** This indicates a **high-value product dependency risk**; the business relies heavily on a small number of expensive items to maintain gross revenue targets.

### 2. Freight Elasticity & Margin Erosion
* **Insight:** Logistics analysis reveals that freight-to-price ratios in bulky categories (e.g., Furniture) exceed 25%, while smaller electronics remain under 8%.
* **Implication:** The current flat-rate or sub-optimized promotional strategy is inefficient for bulk items, suggesting that shipping costs are actively eroding the net profit of heavy inventory.

### 3. Regional Revenue Concentration (Pareto Skew)
* **Insight:** Over 40% of total revenue is concentrated in the SP (São Paulo) region alone.
* **Implication:** Marketing spend in secondary regions is yielding non-linear returns. Resources should be reallocated to optimize the SP supply chain to protect the core revenue base.
